Check out the features available below!

Portability

Need a midi controller in your pocket? Easily connect to your laptop using WiFi or USB (Bluetooth in the works) and enjoy making music wherever you may be.

Ease of Use

The barriers for creating music have been reduced with Beat Remote. Change the scale, key and octave on the fly. Play chords with one button and much more.

Play Surfaces

Beat Remote contains four easy to use play surfaces. Pads - Play notes and launch samples with a familiar 4x3 pad layout. Piano - Like having a full piano to work with? This is for you. Chords - Like the pads screen but each pad can be programmed to play a chord. Control - Contains sliders, dials and an XY controller for controlling your DAW.
